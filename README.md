# Mortgage Calculator

A simple mortgage calculator to visualize your loan over time.

## Features

- Calculate monthly payment
- Calculate total interest
- Calculate total cost
- Visualize your loan over time
- Visualize your portfolio over time with a chosen market return rate
- Compare your mortgage payment to your portfolio growth

## Usage

1. Open the `index.html` file in your browser
2. Enter your loan details
3. View the results
